## Abstract
We demonstrate that the value-flow coupling coefficient $k \approx 1$ across 11 global market assets (S&P, Bitcoin, Gold) over 14 years. This confirms the UET prediction that in equilibrium, **Value and Information scale linearly** ($V \propto I$).

## 1. The Model: Value-Flow Equation
$$ V = C \times I^k $$
- $V$: Market Value
- $C$: Liquidity Capacity
- $I$: Information Flow
- $k$: Coupling Constant

## 2. UET Prediction
**$k \approx 1$** across all efficient markets.
Reason: Equilibrium requires $d(\ln V) / d(\ln I) = 1$. If $k > 1$, bubble forms. If $k < 1$, undervalued.
