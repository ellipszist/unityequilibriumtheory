## 2. Solar Wind (Parker Solar Probe)

Parker discovered "Switchbacks" (magnetic field reversals) in the solar wind.

### UET Interpretation
- Switchbacks = Twisted $I$-field flux tubes (Information vortices)
- Magnetic reconnection = $I$-field topology change
- Coronal heating = Energy released from $I$-field reconnection

This explains the **Coronal Heating Problem**: energy is stored in the $I$-field and released via topology changes.
